declutter meaningfully

Flutter makes parting with objects feel like a celebration. Our platform promotes meaningful decluttering by making the process of giving social. Team flutter is all about passing on objects you know and love to community members you know and love.

1/9
During the initial round of needfinding, we developed a methodology for gaining insights that would shape our quarter-long team project.

From our interviews with individuals of diverse living situations and locations, we found that people value control over objects, low-commitment goals, the ability to retain and share stories, as well as the ability to preserve and build community.
VIEW PRESENTATION
2/9
We revisited the findings from our needfinding, interviewed more participants, and formulated points of view (POVs) for our potential users. From there, we crafted several “How Might We” (HMW) statements to frame our problem area and brainstorm solutions. We created and tested three “experience prototypes” to test assumptions. We found that decluttering sentimental objects benefits from a personal connection to, or reassurance from, the receiver.
